KPI Brief — Levant Tech Solutions
KPI 1: Departmental Salary Expenditure
Definition:
Identifying departments where the total annual salary budget exceeds $150,000 to monitor high-cost centers. (Derived from Q2).

Current value:
Engineering department with a total of $185,000.

Interpretation:
The Engineering department is the primary cost driver. Management should ensure that project delivery and ROI from this department align with this significant investment.

KPI 2: Employee Utilization Rate
Definition:
Tracking the number of "bench" employees who are currently not assigned to any active projects. (Derived from Q7).

Current value:
5 employees are currently unassigned.

Interpretation:
A high number of unassigned employees indicates "under-utilization." This suggests a need for better resource allocation or a push for new project acquisitions to maximize human capital.

KPI 3: Monthly Hiring Momentum
Definition:
Measuring the count of new hires per month to understand the company's expansion speed. (Derived from Q8).

Current value:
Peak hiring occurred in January 2022 (4 hires), with a steady growth of 2 hires per month in early 2025.

Interpretation:
The data shows stable growth. This trend helps HR and Operations plan for workspace capacity, onboarding cycles, and equipment procurement in advance.
